Welcome

Hi, my name is Gloria

I have been passionate about art my entire life, but it is a hobby I put to the side after having a family. Now, I am able to paint more and enjoy customizing pieces with various colors, dimensions, and materials. My art uses acrylic paint covered in Epoxy Resin to give them brightness and protection. Thank you for visiting and feel free to contact me through email for customized orders!
